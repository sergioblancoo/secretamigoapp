App Features

Create the event, add the participants, optionaly add some conditions between participants and finally draw the names.
Send the event invite to each participant with their Secret Amigo. Enjoy!

  • Events

    Choose a name, day, time and other details for your event.

  • Participants

    Add participants to the event. An email address will be required so they can be notified who their Secret Amigo is.

  • Conditions

    Apply some conditions. For example you can select if one of the participants ‘shouldn’t gift to’ someone or ‘should gift to’ a particular participant.

  • Draw

    The app will complete the draw based on conditions previously chosen.

  • Share

    Each participant will receive an email including the name of their Secret Amigo.

  • Enjoy

    Enjoy the event and don’t forget to bring the gift!
